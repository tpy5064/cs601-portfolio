About me

Hello! My name is Tianqi Yang. I am an aspiring font-end web developer with a keen sense of style. I wish to create websites and content as visually appealing as possible with all the functionality too. I enjoy working with React and SCSS and they are the tools of my choice! Please feel free to contact me if you wish to chat!

A little more about myself. I am from China, specifically from the north-eastern region of Inner Mongolia, Hulunbuir. I am also of Manchu descent, a northern Tungusic minority in China. This is how you greet each other in Manchu: ᠰᡳ ᠰᠠᡳᠶᡡᠨ ᠨᡳᠣ (Si sain nio?). Inner Mongolia is a beautiful place where you get to see the vast grasslands, green pasture and herds of sheep. It is a place where you get to be close to nature and experience the hospitality of the locals.

Sadly, Manchu is a critically endangered language and fewer and fewer people are able to speak it. I am doing my part to preserve the language. I would like to teach you, the viewer some phrases if you are interested.
