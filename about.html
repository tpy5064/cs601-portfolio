A little more about myself. I am from China, specifically from the north-eastern region of Inner Mongolia, Hulunbuir. I am also of Manchu descent, a northern ethnic minority in China. This is how you greet each other in Manchu: ᠰᡳ ᠰᠠᡳᠶᡡᠨ ᠨᡳᠣ (Si sain nio?), pronounced "she say-in knee-o?". The Manchu language is written vertically top to bottom, so it isn't displayed in the correct orientation on the web.
